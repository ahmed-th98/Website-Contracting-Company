About

AL-Mawazeen General Contracting Co.

AL-Mawazeen General Contracting Company is a Local Iraqi Company, Based in Basra city, Southern Iraq. The Company is fully registered in Iraq with an ISO Certification and has highly successful track record of quality products and services procured and delivered on time and within costs. AL-Mawazeen provides services and support in different sectors mainly in Oil & Gas, Water & Waste Water treatment system, Power Generation, Mechanical & lighting Materials Supplies, Mechanical and Firefighting works/equipment.
AL-Mawazeen has all required technical resources and supporting facilities to provide this wide range of services, with specialization with design and supply together with Construction and building activities.
